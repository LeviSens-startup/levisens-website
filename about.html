About

Precision Metrology Through Quantum OptoMechanics

LeviSens (Levitated Sensors) is a deep-tech startup pioneering next-generation precision instrumentation based on levitated quantum optomechanics.

LeviSens is committed to bringing quantum-era metrology to industries where precision isn’t optional — it’s mission-critical.

From semiconductor metrology, advanced materials, energy sector, aerospace and defense, we aim to overcome the limitations of conventional probing technologies by delivering measurement solutions that are radically less invasive, faster, and more sensitive.
